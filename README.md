# Mooze

**Mooze is a versatile tool designed to download Spotify songs and other music by locating them on YouTube and facilitating their download—it's as straightforward as that.**

### Features

* **Interactive Menus:** Mooze offers fully interactive menus that can be navigated using either your keyboard or mouse. The interface includes a visual progress bar, allowing users to easily track the status of their downloads in real-time.
* **Custom Audio:** Users have the flexibility to select their preferred audio quality, including various bitrates, as well as the file format for their downloads. This ensures that you can tailor your listening experience to meet your specific needs, whether you prioritize high fidelity or smaller file sizes.
* **Organized Storage:** Mooze allows you to designate a specific folder on your computer for saving downloaded files. This feature helps keep your music library organized, making it easy to locate your favorite tracks without sifting through cluttered directories.
* **Batch Downloading:** With Mooze, you can streamline your music collection by pasting a list of music links to download multiple songs simultaneously. This batch downloading feature saves time and effort, as all selected tracks are neatly archived in your chosen folder.
* **Search Mode:** The built-in search mode enables users to type in keywords to quickly find the exact track they are looking for. This functionality enhances the user experience by making it easier to discover new music or revisit old favorites without hassle.
